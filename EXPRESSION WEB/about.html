Our Story

Welcome to The Written World, a heaven nestled in the heart of Seremban. Founded in 2024, The Written World sprang from a passion for literature and a dream to create a community space where stories are cherished and shared.

Our Mission

We believe in the power of books to enlighten, entertain, and inspire. Our mission is to curate a diverse collection that caters to readers of all ages and interests, fostering a love for reading in our community.

What We Offer

From the latest bestsellers to timeless classics, The Written World offers a carefully selected range of titles across various genres. We also host author events, book clubs, and reading workshops, making us more than just a bookshop – we're a literary hub. Besides books, we aim to offer the best quality of stationery in the market, both online and offline, which you'll hardly find elsewhere!

Why Choose Us

We ensure that our main focus is on the quality of the products. We will make sure that customers get the best quality from us. Plus, behind the shelves, our dedicated team of bibliophiles works tirelessly to recommend the perfect read. We’re proud to have knowledgeable staff who share our vision and contribute to our warm, welcoming atmosphere.

How to Contact Us

We invite you to visit us and explore our collection. If you have any doubts, suggestions, or comments about us, our products, or our site, do not hesitate to contact us! We can be reached through info@thewrittenworld.my. We’ll get back to you within 24 hours. Thank you for your patient and support.
