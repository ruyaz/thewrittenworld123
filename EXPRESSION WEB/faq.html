Frequently Asked Questions

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit and debit cards, as well as PayPal and bank transfers.

How can I track my order?

Once your order has been shipped, you will receive an email with the tracking number and a link to track your shipment.

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we offer international shipping. Please check our shipping page for details on countries we ship to and shipping fees.

Can I cancel my order?

You can cancel your order within 24 hours of placing it. Please contact us immediately for assistance.

Do you offer gift cards?

Yes, we offer gift cards of varying denominations. You can purchase them through our website or in-store.

What is your return policy?

We accept returns within 30 days of purchase. The item must be in original condition, and you must provide proof of purchase. Please visit our returns page for more details.
